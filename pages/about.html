this site

i tried to keep things as simple as possible. this means:

  • vanilla html, css, js
  • web server written in C1
  • art drawn in Paint

which in turn, means:

  • no dependencies or packages to deal with2
  • no complicated setup to run server3. just one executable
  • no reliance on external platforms/providers4

and most importantly:

  • blazingly fast
  • 2kb gzipped
  • made with love

but really:

  • a fun project, and
  • a good learning experience!

and that's all there is to it. if you're interested in more details, feel free to view the github repo or email me at kevin@kevinjlee.xyz
